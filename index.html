Welcome

I'm Robin!

Welcome to my personal webpage! I am currently a 'Hybrid mobile app developer' trainee, following a 6 month course given by VDAB & Multimedi. In the previous years, I have obtained a college degree in Pharmaceutical and Biological labtechnology and I used to work as a labtechnician for company's like Pfizer, Galapagos and Agilent Technologies.

Why the sudden change? Being a labtechnician was fun, but I missed the challenge of being confronted with different kinds of issues. Also I am very eager to learn but was limited in my creativity due to strict pharmaceutical protocols. During my career in the lab, I learned a few important and interesting skills that could come of good use in my search for a new challenge in IT! Skills like good documentation practices, working in a team and being someone who picks up new technologies quite easily.

Personally I'm looking for a company where I can both grow as a developer and as a person. So if you want to get to know me better, take a look around and maybe we can get in touch!
